About Incubation Cell

Ideas to Incubate

We help to grow your business with Right Support & from Innovate to Incubate

Incubation Cell has been established to promote innovation and entrepreneurship in the Medi-Caps University.To accomplish its goal, Incubation Cell runs and manages a Incubator called INCUBATION Center & Development of Entrepreneurship & Innovation Support Incubation Cell established at Medi-Caps University.


Incubation Cell facilitates incubation of new enterprises with innovative technologies by admitting them at its Incubation Centre and provides physical, technical, financial and networking supports and services.
